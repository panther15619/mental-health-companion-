Mental Health Companion

Our mission is to create a safe, anonymous space where students and individuals can share their thoughts, find supportive words, and access positive resources. We believe that mental health matters, and with time this platform will grow into a mobile app so support is always in your pocket.

About Us

Mental Health Companion was created with the belief that nobody should feel alone in their struggles. By combining technology, community, and compassion, our platform provides a safe place for anyone to express themselves without judgment. Whether you're seeking comfort, sharing positivity, or simply reading kind words, you are welcome here.

How It Works

  1. Step 1: Share your thoughts anonymously in the venting area.
  2. Step 2: Read uplifting posts on the Wall of Positivity.
  3. Step 3: Discover resources for mental health and self-care.
  4. Step 4: Stay tuned for our upcoming mobile app to take support everywhere you go.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this platform really anonymous?
A: Yes. We do not collect names or personal data. Your posts stay private.

Q: Will my posts be saved?
A: Posts are stored locally on your device. No one else can see them yet.

Q: Will there be a mobile app?
A: Yes! A mobile app is in development to make support even more accessible.
